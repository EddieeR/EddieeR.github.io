Paris has traditionally played the role of Europe’s ‘old soul’. While literary romanticism and a deep sense of history lingers in the air, Parisian men are channeling a more nonchalant, je ne sais quoi approach to contemporary style. Les parisiens have today crafted a seamless blend of France’s high-fashion ancestry – think Balmain, Saint Laurent and Dior Homme – with newcomer labels A.P.C, Ami and Maison Kitsuné
